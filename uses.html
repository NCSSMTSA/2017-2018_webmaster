Why Robots?


Efficiency

Robots with AI can be used to perform repetitive tasks at a higher, and more effective rate than humans. The speed of performance and quality of product are based only on the parameters its creator sets for them, and thus robots will be able to work faster than humans.


Keep Us From Danger

Robots can also be sent on dangerous missions that would usually place the life of a human at risk. For example, if a robot with AI were to be sent on a rescue mission, it would be unaffected mentally by environmental factors and would most likely calculate the most effective route to the stranded individual. Robots can also be sent on deep space missions as well, flying through space for as long as they were built to last, completely unaffected by isolation factors that would affect human pilots.


Consumer Benefits

By creating more bots with AI, there can be huge benefits for the consumer too. The most obvious of these benefits would be that wait times for businesses would be cut down, as companies could mass produce AI bots to interact with humans to address their needs.


Education

In education, AI can be fed with student information to produce a learning plan tailored to that student. As the student develops and learns, the AI will learn as well, and based on how the AI is created, it may be able to learn and adjust its plan to assist with the student's learning. On a broader scale, AI can also help create learning plans for entire classes by analyzing class data and identifying “instruction gaps” in the teacher's plan. Thus AI can be used to maximize education quality through its ability to adjust to changing situations.
