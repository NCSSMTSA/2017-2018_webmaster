Robot Safety


Human Workforce

A colossal concern of AI is its potential to send humans into unemployment. Due to the incredible intelligence of AI robots, it will be increasingly difficult to compete with their efficiency and speed. Employers will begin to choose to spend an upfront cost for a robot that could last decades (with heightened efficiency) instead of a long-term cost for “slow” humans.


Ethics

The ethical argument against AI is derived from the fact that robots lack emotion. This unfortunately limits them to logical decisions, and it can be argued that perhaps robots do not always make the best judgement call. Will humans be able to program morals into AI?


Liabilities

In 2016, a Tesla autopilot AI mistook a white truck for the sky, resulting in an accident that ended up killing the driver. Here-in lies the problem—who is liable for this incident? It is easy to turn to the company as being the liable party for the incedent, but how long will it be before companies cannot control the AI that they have created?
