Meet our Robot

Introducing the Eco-Bot


What is it ?

The Eco-Bot is short for Eco-Friendly Robot and it belongs to a class of energetically sustainable robots that is capable of helping its owner to become much more sustainable than ever before.


What can it do?

This robot is capable of collecting energy from the sun through solar panels and waste matter in the environment to self-sustain itself.


Does it pollute?

The by-product from this process, which is similar to phtosynthesis, is oxygen, which will benefit us humans and the environment.

Benefits of AI


Why not use humans?

Unlike humans, robots cannot retire. This means that they are able to work at a higher efficiency and only require occasional maintenance, which usually can easily be fixed. Thus, in this way robots with artificial intelligence are preferable over their human counterparts, as they are only limited by the parameters built into them.

Robots with artificial intelligence are more rational than humans. Though some may see the lack of emotion in a robot as a downside, it may possibly be beneficial, as the robot would not be clouded by emotion, enabling it to make the most logical decision possible.



AI Drives Business


Services our AI offers

  • Team-Creation Services
    • This service creates teams based on the how different team members have worked previously. It takes into account both individual skills and past team success to create new groups of team members where success can be more likely.
  • Teacher-Evaluation Services
    • This service keeps teachers under check, by recording classroom activities. It uses our state-of-the-art algorithm to determine how well teachers are lecturing. It also allows people to put learning aids like powerpoints in the system and determine the effectiveness of the teachers’ teaching styles. It also provides suggestions for teachers to improve their teaching methods.
  • Student Comprehension and Learning Styles Services
    • This service can allow teachers to learn more about student understanding through methods other than classic assessment techniques like tests. It can grade audio recordings, video explanations, and open-ended activities.
  • Business Decisions Services
    • This service uses business plans and ideas that you already have used and runs our algorithm to decide which decisions in the past have been productive or unproductive. It then recommends further steps you can take to lead your business successfully.
  • Worker Training Services
    • This service trains workers using modules. It gauges worker understanding throughout the training process through open-ended activities like decision making activities. It then calculates how much a worker has learned from the modules and creates new modules which can help the worker learn the rest of the information properly.
