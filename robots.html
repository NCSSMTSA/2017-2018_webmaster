Meet Eco-Bot!

Introducing the Eco-Bot.

  • The Eco-Bot is short for Eco-Friendly Robot and it belongs to a class of energetically sustainable robots that is capable of helping its owner to become a much more sustainable then before.
  • This robot is capable of collecting energy from the sun through solar panels and waste matter in the environment to self-sustain itself.
  • The by-product from this process, which is similar to phtosynthesis, is oxygen, which will benefit us humans and the environment.

Despite this functionality, Eco-Bot also has great hidden features! These features include:

  1. Learn how to teach and adapt teaching based on a student's needs.
  2. Create new learning modules for students based on the questions they missed.
  3. Learn different team members work together in a business or school project and pair people.
  4. Decide business plans based on past business plans and their success/failure.
  5. Created worker training based on worker's past experiences and weaknesses.
  6. Create pricing system based on customer data.
